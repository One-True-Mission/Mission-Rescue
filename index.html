How This Website Works

Welcome to our mission! This site makes it easy to help rescue animals and connect them with loving homes. To get started, simply click the green “Adopt a Puppy • Help Rescue an Animal” button above—then choose Mansfield or Grand Prairie shelters to view adoptable pets.

Want to know more about the heart behind this mission? Visit our About page in the menu to read how we’re working to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need.

Be sure to check out New Pet Resources for trusted links and tips to keep puppies and kittens healthy, safe, and thriving.

Not ready to adopt? You can still make a difference! Click the “Click Here to Support” button to visit our clothing store. Every purchase helps donate 15% of profits directly back to local animal shelters.
